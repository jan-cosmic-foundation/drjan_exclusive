Registration Fee: GHS 600 per adult (mandatory)

This covers:

  • Meals (main meal across 10 days)
  • Venue and conference costs
  • Teaching materials
  • ID cards and administration
  • Publicity and general retreat overheads

Important Notes:

  • Accommodation is not included in the registration fee.
  • Participants must arrange and pay for accommodation separately.

Accommodation will be hosted at Windy Lodge Beach Resort, offering a range of options to support affordability:

  • Rates range from GHS 120 to GHS 750 per night
  • Limited availability across each pricing tier

To support accessibility:

  • Room sharing will be permitted (e.g. 2 adults sharing, or families sharing larger rooms)
  • Larger rooms (e.g. queen-sized) may accommodate up to 4 people

Allocation Approach:

  • Rooms will be assigned on a first-come, first-served basis
  • Allocation confirmed only upon full payment

Catering will be provided by Patty’s Delish, aligned with JCF’s vegan dietary principles.

  • One main vegan meal per day
  • Cost: GHS 20–30 per person per day

Breakfast Provision:

  • Light self-service options (tea, coffee, bread) will be available

Support Considerations:

Efforts will be made to:

  • Support children attending
  • Assist brothers and sisters who are volunteering but financially constrained

This will be supported through:

  • Registration income
  • Voluntary donations
